## 💰 REVENUE STREAMS (Complete Breakdown)

### 1. 📢 **SKY ADS** (Self-Serve Advertising)

**Model:** Subscription-based recurring revenue

| Package | Price (USD) | Price (BRL) | Features |
|---------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| **Foundation** | $97/mo | R$497/mo | • 2 Rooftop Signs<br>• ~63K impressions/mo per sign<br>• Real-time stats dashboard<br>• Cancel anytime |
| **Skyline** | $197/mo | R$997/mo | • 2 Rooftop Signs<br>• Blimp (flying LED display)<br>• LED Wrap on buildings<br>• Plane with banner<br>• 200K+ combined impressions/mo |
| **Landmark** | Custom | Custom | • Everything in Skyline<br>• Custom 3D building with brand<br>• Social media launch posts<br>• Dedicated account management |

**Ad Formats:**

| Format | CTR | Description |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| **Rooftop Sign** | 0.93% | Illuminated 360° spinning sign on top-contributor buildings. Direct click-to-link. |
| **Blimp** | 0.35% | Airship with LED screens on both sides. Flies slowly across skyline. |
| **Plane Banner** | ~0.40% | Traditional banner plane flying across city. |
| **LED Wrap** | ~0.60% | Building facade wrapped in branded LED display. |
| **Landmark Building** | 1.72% | Custom 3D building with full brand integration (top performer). |

**Key Metrics:**
- Average CTR: 1%+ (3x industry average of 0.33%)
- Click-to-visit rate: 96%
- Impressions per rooftop sign: ~63K/month
- Conversion rate (example): 6 users to Hobby trial from 1,400 impressions in 24h

**Tech:** Stripe integration, self-serve dashboard, real-time analytics

**Revenue Potential:**
- 64 ads sold × $97 avg = **$6,208 MRR** (conservative)
- At 200 ads: **$19,400 MRR**
- At 500 ads: **$48,500 MRR**

### 3. 🛍️ **SHOP** (Digital Goods & Customization)

**Model:** One-time purchases for building customization

**Product Categories:**

| Category | Items | Price Range |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| **Effects** | Auras, particles, glows, animations | $5 - $50 |
| **Structures** | Antennas, satellites, rooftop additions | $10 - $100 |
| **Identity Items** | Crowns, badges, special markers | $3 - $30 |
| **Face Decorations** | Windows, doors, entrances | $5 - $25 |
| **Seasonal Items** | Holiday themes, limited editions | $10 - $75 |

**Purchase Flow:**
1. User signs in with GitHub
2. Claims their building (must have active GitHub profile)
3. Browses shop catalog
4. Selects items, pays via Stripe
5. Items instantly applied to 3D building
6. Visible to all city visitors

**Gifting System:**
- Send items to other developers
- Includes personalized message
- Creates social engagement loop

**Revenue Potential:**
- 100 purchases/day × $25 avg = **$75,000/mo**
- 500 purchases/day × $25 avg = **$375,000/mo**
- Conservative (50/day): **$37,500/mo**

## ⚠️ RISKS & MITIGATION

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| **GitHub API Changes** | High | Diversify auth (GitLab, Bitbucket) |
| **Vercel/Supabase Price Increases** | Medium | Multi-cloud strategy, self-host option |
| **Ad Blocker Usage** | Medium | Native ad formats (buildings, not banners) |
| **Developer Burnout** | High | Automate moderation, hire support |
| **Competition** | Medium | First-mover advantage, network effects |
| **Economic Downturn** | High | Diversify revenue (B2C + B2B) |
| **Privacy Regulations** | Medium | GDPR/CCPA compliance, transparent policies |
